# NovaTrust Bank
## Cybersecurity Governance Program
### Module 10 – ISO 27001 Statement of Applicability (SoA)

---

# 1. Introduction

The Statement of Applicability (SoA) defines the security controls implemented by NovaTrust Bank to manage information security risks.

This document links the enterprise cyber risk assessment and risk treatment plan to the security controls defined in the ISO/IEC 27001:2022 standard.

The SoA identifies:

• which ISO 27001 controls are applicable  
• justification for including or excluding controls  
• the implementation status of each control  
• the risk mitigation objectives supported by each control

Maintaining a Statement of Applicability ensures transparency in how NovaTrust Bank addresses cybersecurity risks.

---

# 2. Purpose of the Statement of Applicability

The Statement of Applicability serves several important purposes within the cybersecurity governance framework.

These include:

• demonstrating compliance with ISO 27001 requirements  
• documenting security controls implemented by the organization  
• linking security controls to identified cyber risks  
• providing evidence for regulatory and certification audits

The SoA provides a structured overview of the bank's cybersecurity control environment.

---

# 3. ISO 27001 Control Structure

ISO/IEC 27001:2022 defines 93 information security controls organized into four categories.

| Control Category | Description |
|------------------|-------------|
| Organizational Controls | Governance, policies, and risk management |
| People Controls | Employee security awareness and responsibilities |
| Physical Controls | Physical protection of facilities and infrastructure |
| Technological Controls | Technical safeguards for systems and networks |

NovaTrust Bank implements controls across all four domains to maintain a comprehensive cybersecurity program.

---

# 4. Control Applicability Criteria

Controls are considered applicable if they:

• mitigate identified cybersecurity risks  
• protect critical banking systems  
• support regulatory compliance  
• strengthen operational resilience

Controls may be excluded only if they are not relevant to the organization's operational environment.

All exclusions must be justified within this document.

---

# 6. Control Implementation Status

Security controls are categorized based on their implementation maturity.

| Status | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Implemented | Control fully operational |
| Partially Implemented | Control implemented but requires improvement |
| Planned | Control scheduled for implementation |

NovaTrust Bank maintains continuous improvement of its control environment to address evolving cyber threats.

---

# 7. Link Between Risks and Controls

Each security control implemented within NovaTrust Bank addresses one or more cyber risks identified in the enterprise risk register.

Examples include:

• ransomware risk mitigated through endpoint protection and backup controls  
• credential compromise mitigated through multi-factor authentication  
• vendor compromise mitigated through supplier security controls

This linkage ensures that security controls directly address organizational risk exposure.

---

# 8. Maintenance of the Statement of Applicability

The Statement of Applicability is reviewed periodically to ensure it reflects the current cybersecurity environment.

Updates occur when:

• new risks are identified  
• new systems are introduced  
• regulatory requirements change  
• new security controls are implemented

Regular updates ensure that the SoA remains accurate and relevant.

---

# 9. Role in ISO 27001 Certification

The Statement of Applicability is a core document required for ISO 27001 certification audits.

During certification assessments, auditors review the SoA to verify that:

• controls address identified risks  
• security controls are properly implemented  
• governance processes align with ISO requirements

Maintaining an accurate SoA demonstrates NovaTrust Bank’s commitment to international cybersecurity standards.

---

# 10. Strategic Importance of the SoA

The Statement of Applicability ensures that NovaTrust Bank maintains a transparent and structured approach to cybersecurity governance.

By clearly documenting implemented security controls, the SoA enables the organization to:

• demonstrate regulatory compliance  
• manage cyber risk effectively  
• support continuous security improvement  
• strengthen customer trust in digital banking services
