# NovaTrust Bank
## Cybersecurity Governance Program
### Module 03 – Security Governance Structure

---

# 1. Introduction

Effective cybersecurity governance is essential for protecting financial systems, maintaining regulatory compliance, and ensuring operational resilience.

NovaTrust Bank has established a structured cybersecurity governance model that defines leadership responsibilities, accountability, and oversight mechanisms for managing cyber risk across the organization.

This governance framework ensures that cybersecurity is aligned with business objectives and integrated into enterprise risk management processes.

---

# 2. Governance Principles

The cybersecurity governance program at NovaTrust Bank is based on the following principles:

• Clear accountability for cybersecurity leadership  
• Alignment between cybersecurity and business strategy  
• Independent oversight of cyber risk management  
• Continuous monitoring and reporting of cybersecurity performance  
• Compliance with financial sector regulatory expectations  

These principles ensure that cybersecurity decisions are transparent, accountable, and aligned with organizational objectives.

---

# 3. Governance Structure Overview

Cybersecurity governance at NovaTrust Bank follows a structured hierarchy that ensures oversight at the board and executive levels.

Governance structure:

Board of Directors  
↓  
Board Risk Committee  
↓  
Chief Executive Officer (CEO)  
↓  
Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)  
↓  
Cybersecurity Governance & Risk Team  
↓  
Security Operations & IT Teams

This structure ensures that cybersecurity risks are escalated appropriately and managed effectively across the organization.

---

# 4. Board of Directors

The Board of Directors holds ultimate accountability for cybersecurity governance within NovaTrust Bank.

The board ensures that cybersecurity risks are appropriately managed and aligned with the organization's risk appetite.

Key responsibilities include:

• Providing strategic oversight of cybersecurity risk  
• Approving cybersecurity governance policies  
• Reviewing cybersecurity risk reports  
• Ensuring sufficient investment in cybersecurity capabilities  
• Monitoring regulatory compliance

The board receives periodic cybersecurity briefings from the Chief Information Security Officer.

---

# 5. Board Risk Committee

The Board Risk Committee is responsible for overseeing enterprise risk management, including cyber risk.

This committee provides focused oversight of cybersecurity issues and ensures that cyber risks are integrated into the organization's broader risk management framework.

Responsibilities include:

• Reviewing cyber risk assessments  
• Monitoring cybersecurity performance metrics  
• Reviewing major cybersecurity incidents  
• Evaluating cybersecurity investments  
• Ensuring alignment with regulatory expectations

The committee reports its findings and recommendations to the Board of Directors.

---

# 6. Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

The Chief Executive Officer is responsible for ensuring that cybersecurity governance is integrated into the organization's overall business strategy.

The CEO ensures that adequate resources and leadership support are provided for cybersecurity initiatives.

Responsibilities include:

• Supporting cybersecurity governance initiatives  
• Ensuring cross-department collaboration on security matters  
• Ensuring organizational accountability for cyber risk management  
• Supporting incident response during major cyber events

---

# 7. Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)

The Chief Information Security Officer leads the cybersecurity program at NovaTrust Bank.

The CISO is responsible for developing, implementing, and maintaining the organization's cybersecurity strategy and security controls.

Key responsibilities include:

• Developing cybersecurity strategy and policies  
• Managing cyber risk assessment processes  
• Leading incident response activities  
• Reporting cyber risk metrics to executive leadership  
• Ensuring compliance with regulatory and industry standards

The CISO provides regular cybersecurity reports to the Board Risk Committee.

---

# 8. Cybersecurity Governance and Risk Team

The Cybersecurity Governance and Risk Team is responsible for implementing cybersecurity governance processes across the organization.

This team supports the CISO by performing risk management activities and ensuring compliance with security frameworks.

Responsibilities include:

• Conducting cyber risk assessments  
• Maintaining the enterprise risk register  
• Monitoring compliance with security policies  
• Supporting regulatory audits and assessments  
• Managing third-party cybersecurity risk

---

# 9. Security Operations Team

The Security Operations Team is responsible for the day-to-day monitoring and protection of NovaTrust Bank's systems and networks.

Key activities include:

• Monitoring security events and alerts  
• Investigating potential cyber incidents  
• Responding to cybersecurity threats  
• Managing security technologies and monitoring tools  
• Supporting incident response activities

This team works closely with the Governance and Risk Team to ensure that operational security aligns with governance policies.

---

# 10. Information Technology Team

The Information Technology team is responsible for implementing and maintaining secure infrastructure across the organization.

Responsibilities include:

• Maintaining secure system configurations  
• Managing patching and vulnerability remediation  
• Implementing identity and access management systems  
• Supporting secure infrastructure operations

The IT team collaborates with the Security Operations team to maintain secure banking infrastructure.

---

# 11. Cybersecurity Reporting and Escalation

NovaTrust Bank maintains structured cybersecurity reporting channels to ensure timely escalation of cyber risks and incidents.

Reporting mechanisms include:

• Monthly cybersecurity risk reports to executive leadership  
• Quarterly cybersecurity briefings to the Board Risk Committee  
• Immediate escalation of major cybersecurity incidents

These reporting channels ensure that leadership remains informed of cybersecurity risks and organizational security posture.

---

# 12. Integration with Enterprise Risk Management

Cybersecurity risk management is integrated into NovaTrust Bank's broader enterprise risk management framework.

Cyber risks are assessed, monitored, and managed alongside other operational and financial risks.

This integration ensures that cybersecurity decisions align with the organization's overall risk management strategy.

---

# 13. Importance of Cybersecurity Governance

Strong cybersecurity governance ensures that NovaTrust Bank can effectively manage cyber risks while maintaining secure banking services.

This governance structure enables:

• clear accountability for cybersecurity leadership  
• effective oversight of cyber risk  
• rapid response to emerging threats  
• regulatory compliance  

Through this governance framework, NovaTrust Bank maintains a mature and resilient cybersecurity posture.
