# NovaTrust Bank
## Cybersecurity Governance Program
### Module 16 – Security Awareness & Human Risk Management

---

# 1. Introduction

Human behavior plays a critical role in cybersecurity. Many cyber incidents occur not because of technical failures, but due to human errors such as phishing attacks, weak passwords, or accidental disclosure of sensitive information.

NovaTrust Bank implements a comprehensive security awareness and human risk management program designed to educate employees, strengthen security culture, and reduce human-related cyber risks.

This program ensures that employees understand their responsibilities in protecting the bank's information assets and financial systems.

---

# 2. Objectives of Security Awareness

The security awareness program aims to achieve the following objectives:

• educate employees about cybersecurity threats  
• reduce the risk of phishing and social engineering attacks  
• encourage secure handling of financial and customer data  
• promote responsible use of banking systems and technology  
• strengthen the overall security culture within NovaTrust Bank

These objectives support the broader cybersecurity governance strategy.

---

# 3. Common Human-Related Cyber Risks

Human-related cyber risks commonly encountered within financial institutions include:

| Risk | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| Phishing Attacks | Employees tricked into revealing login credentials |
| Social Engineering | Attackers manipulating employees to bypass security controls |
| Weak Passwords | Poor password practices leading to account compromise |
| Accidental Data Disclosure | Unintentional sharing of confidential data |
| Insider Threats | Employees intentionally misusing system access |

The awareness program helps mitigate these risks through training and policy enforcement.

---

# 4. Security Awareness Training Program

NovaTrust Bank provides regular cybersecurity training to all employees.

Training topics include:

• phishing awareness and email security  
• password management best practices  
• secure handling of customer financial data  
• identification of suspicious activity  
• reporting cybersecurity incidents

Training sessions are conducted during employee onboarding and repeated periodically to reinforce security practices.

---

# 5. Phishing Simulation Exercises

To strengthen employee awareness, NovaTrust Bank conducts simulated phishing exercises.

These exercises involve sending controlled phishing emails to employees to test their ability to identify suspicious messages.

Outcomes of phishing simulations include:

• measuring employee susceptibility to phishing attacks  
• identifying departments requiring additional training  
• improving employee awareness of social engineering threats

Simulation results are used to improve training programs.

---

# 6. Insider Risk Management

Insider threats can originate from employees or contractors who misuse their access privileges.

NovaTrust Bank reduces insider risk through several mechanisms:

• strict access control policies  
• monitoring of privileged user activities  
• separation of duties for critical financial systems  
• background checks for sensitive roles

These controls reduce the risk of intentional misuse of banking systems.

---

# 7. Secure Behavior Policies

Employees are required to follow security policies designed to protect banking systems and customer data.

Examples of secure behavior policies include:

• use of strong passwords and multi-factor authentication  
• prohibition of sharing login credentials  
• secure handling of financial data  
• reporting suspicious emails or activity

Compliance with these policies is mandatory for all employees.

---

# 8. Security Culture Development

NovaTrust Bank promotes a strong security culture where cybersecurity is considered everyone's responsibility.

Security culture initiatives include:

• awareness campaigns  
• regular security communications  
• leadership support for cybersecurity initiatives  
• recognition of employees who demonstrate good security practices

A strong security culture helps reinforce safe behaviors across the organization.

---

# 9. Reporting Security Concerns

Employees are encouraged to report suspicious activity or potential security incidents.

Reporting mechanisms include:

• dedicated security reporting channels  
• internal incident reporting systems  
• communication with the security operations team

Encouraging prompt reporting enables faster detection of cyber threats.

---

# 10. Measuring Security Awareness Effectiveness

NovaTrust Bank evaluates the effectiveness of its security awareness program using several metrics.

Key indicators include:

| Metric | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Training Completion Rate | Percentage of employees completing security training |
| Phishing Simulation Success Rate | Percentage of employees correctly identifying phishing emails |
| Incident Reporting Rate | Number of security incidents reported by employees |
| Policy Compliance Rate | Employee adherence to security policies |

These metrics help improve the security awareness program over time.

---

# 11. Continuous Improvement of Human Risk Management

Cyber threats evolve continuously, requiring ongoing improvements to employee awareness programs.

NovaTrust Bank regularly updates training materials to address new threats such as:

• advanced phishing campaigns  
• AI-generated social engineering attacks  
• emerging fraud techniques

Continuous improvement ensures that employees remain prepared to recognize evolving cyber threats.

---

# 12. Importance of Security Awareness in Financial Institutions

Financial institutions handle highly sensitive financial data and must maintain strong security practices across all employees.

An effective security awareness program enables NovaTrust Bank to:

• reduce human-related cyber risks  
• strengthen overall cybersecurity posture  
• protect customer financial information  
• support regulatory compliance

By empowering employees with cybersecurity knowledge, NovaTrust Bank creates a stronger defense against cyber threats.
