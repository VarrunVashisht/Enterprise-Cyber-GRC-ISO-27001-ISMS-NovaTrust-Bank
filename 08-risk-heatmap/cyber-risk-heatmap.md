# NovaTrust Bank
## Cybersecurity Governance Program
### Module 08 – Cyber Risk Heatmap

---

# 1. Introduction

Cyber risk heatmaps provide a visual representation of cybersecurity risks based on their likelihood and potential impact. This visualization helps executive leadership and board members quickly understand the organization's cyber risk exposure.

NovaTrust Bank uses risk heatmaps to prioritize cybersecurity investments, guide risk mitigation strategies, and support executive-level decision making.

The heatmap is derived from the enterprise cyber risk assessment conducted in Module 07.

---

# 2. Purpose of the Cyber Risk Heatmap

The cyber risk heatmap supports strategic cybersecurity governance by:

• highlighting the most significant cyber risks  
• enabling leadership to understand organizational risk exposure  
• prioritizing mitigation efforts  
• supporting board-level cyber risk reporting  

Visualizing risk levels helps leadership focus attention on the most critical cybersecurity threats facing NovaTrust Bank.

---

# 3. Risk Heatmap Methodology

The risk heatmap plots each risk according to two dimensions:

Likelihood – probability of the risk occurring  
Impact – severity of consequences if the risk occurs

Each risk is assigned a score using the risk scoring model:

Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact

These scores are then mapped onto the heatmap to determine overall risk severity.

---

# 4. Risk Heatmap Matrix

NovaTrust Bank uses a standard five-by-five risk matrix.

Impact is represented on the vertical axis and likelihood on the horizontal axis.

| Impact / Likelihood | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 |
|--------------------|---|---|---|---|---|
| 5 Critical Impact | Medium | High | High | Critical | Critical |
| 4 Major Impact | Medium | Medium | High | High | Critical |
| 3 Moderate Impact | Low | Medium | Medium | High | High |
| 2 Minor Impact | Low | Low | Medium | Medium | Medium |
| 1 Minimal Impact | Low | Low | Low | Low | Medium |

# 6. Key Cyber Risk Observations

Analysis of the cyber risk heatmap indicates that several risks fall within the critical and high-risk categories.

Critical risks include:

• ransomware targeting core banking infrastructure  
• credential compromise through phishing attacks  
• payment fraud affecting customer accounts  
• denial-of-service attacks disrupting online banking

High risks include:

• cloud security misconfiguration  
• insider misuse of privileged access  
• third-party supply chain compromise  
• API vulnerabilities in banking services

These risks represent priority areas for cybersecurity investment and mitigation.

---

# 7. Strategic Risk Priorities

Based on the heatmap analysis, NovaTrust Bank prioritizes the following cybersecurity focus areas:

1. Identity and access security improvements  
2. Advanced ransomware detection and response capabilities  
3. Strong third-party security oversight  
4. Cloud security monitoring and configuration management  
5. Enhanced fraud detection mechanisms

These strategic initiatives support the bank’s broader cyber resilience strategy.

---

# 8. Executive Cyber Risk Reporting

Cyber risk heatmaps are presented to the Board Risk Committee as part of regular cybersecurity reporting.

Executive reports typically include:

• current cyber risk heatmap  
• changes in risk levels over time  
• mitigation progress for critical risks  
• cybersecurity investment priorities

This reporting enables leadership to maintain oversight of cyber risk exposure.

---

# 9. Continuous Risk Monitoring

The cyber risk heatmap is updated periodically as part of NovaTrust Bank’s cyber risk management process.

Updates occur when:

• new cyber threats emerge  
• significant technology changes occur  
• security incidents reveal new vulnerabilities  
• risk mitigation measures reduce risk levels

Regular updates ensure that leadership has an accurate understanding of the bank’s cyber risk posture.

---

# 10. Importance of Cyber Risk Visualization

Risk visualization helps translate technical cybersecurity risks into business-level insights that executives can easily understand.

By presenting cyber risks visually, NovaTrust Bank ensures that cybersecurity governance remains transparent and aligned with strategic business priorities.
