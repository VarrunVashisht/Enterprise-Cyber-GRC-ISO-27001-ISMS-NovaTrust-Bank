# NovaTrust Bank
## Enterprise Cybersecurity Governance Program
### Module 01 – Enterprise Overview

---

# 1. Organization Profile

NovaTrust Bank is a digital-first financial institution headquartered in Melbourne, Australia. The bank provides modern financial services through secure digital platforms including mobile banking, online banking, and integrated digital payment systems.

The organization serves approximately 1.8 million customers globally and processes millions of financial transactions daily. NovaTrust Bank relies heavily on secure digital infrastructure to deliver reliable financial services to customers, businesses, and financial partners.

As a financial institution, NovaTrust Bank handles highly sensitive information including customer financial records, payment transactions, personal identification data, and internal banking systems. Protection of this information is essential to maintaining customer trust, meeting regulatory obligations, and ensuring operational resilience.

Cybersecurity governance is therefore a strategic priority for NovaTrust Bank. The cybersecurity program ensures that digital banking services remain secure, resilient, and compliant with financial sector regulations.

---

# 7. Cybersecurity Objectives

The cybersecurity program for NovaTrust Bank is designed to support the organization's strategic and operational goals.

Key cybersecurity objectives include:

1. Protect customer financial information and sensitive data
2. Prevent cyber attacks targeting banking infrastructure
3. Ensure continuous availability of digital banking services
4. Detect and respond rapidly to security incidents
5. Maintain compliance with financial sector regulations
6. Strengthen customer trust through strong security governance

# 8. Cybersecurity Framework Alignment

NovaTrust Bank's cybersecurity governance program aligns with globally recognized cybersecurity frameworks and regulatory expectations.

These frameworks provide structured guidance for managing cyber risk in financial institutions.

Key frameworks include:

• ISO/IEC 27001:2022 – Information Security Management System  
• NIST Cybersecurity Framework – Risk-based cybersecurity governance  
• Financial sector regulatory requirements  

Alignment with these frameworks ensures that NovaTrust Bank maintains a mature cybersecurity posture capable of addressing modern cyber threats.

---

# 9. Importance of Cybersecurity for Financial Institutions

Financial institutions are prime targets for cyber criminals due to the direct financial value of banking systems and customer financial data.

Common threats targeting banks include:

• Ransomware attacks  
• Payment fraud schemes  
• Credential theft and account takeover  
• Supply chain attacks through vendors  
• Insider threats  

A robust cybersecurity governance program ensures that NovaTrust Bank can effectively manage these threats while maintaining secure and reliable financial services.

---

# 10. Strategic Importance of Cybersecurity Governance

Cybersecurity governance enables NovaTrust Bank to manage cyber risk in a structured and accountable manner.

Strong governance ensures that:

- Security responsibilities are clearly defined across the organization
- Cyber risks are identified, assessed, and managed effectively
- Security controls are implemented consistently across systems and processes
- Security incidents are detected, responded to, and resolved efficiently
- Regulatory and compliance obligations are fully met

This governance framework ensures that cybersecurity supports NovaTrust Bank's long-term strategic objectives while protecting customers and financial systems.
