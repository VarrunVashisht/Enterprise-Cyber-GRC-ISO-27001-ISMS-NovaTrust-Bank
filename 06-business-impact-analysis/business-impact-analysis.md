# NovaTrust Bank
## Cybersecurity Governance Program
### Module 06 – Business Impact Analysis (BIA)

---

# 1. Introduction

Business Impact Analysis (BIA) is a structured process used to identify critical business services and evaluate the potential consequences of system disruptions.

For financial institutions such as NovaTrust Bank, maintaining continuous access to banking services is essential for customer trust, financial stability, and regulatory compliance.

The purpose of this Business Impact Analysis is to:

• identify critical banking services  
• evaluate operational and financial impacts of service disruptions  
• determine recovery priorities  
• establish recovery time objectives (RTO) and recovery point objectives (RPO)

The results of this analysis support NovaTrust Bank’s operational resilience strategy and disaster recovery planning.

---

# 2. Importance of Business Impact Analysis in Banking

Financial institutions operate systems that support millions of daily financial transactions. Even a short disruption can have significant consequences.

Potential impacts of service disruption include:

• inability for customers to access funds  
• disruption of payment processing  
• financial loss due to transaction delays  
• reputational damage  
• regulatory penalties

By identifying critical services and acceptable recovery times, NovaTrust Bank can ensure that essential banking services remain operational even during cyber incidents or system failures.

---

# 3. Critical Business Services

NovaTrust Bank delivers several essential services that must remain available to customers and financial partners.

The following services are identified as critical business functions.

| Business Service | Description | Criticality |
|------------------|-------------|------------|
| Core Banking Operations | Management of customer accounts and transactions | Critical |
| Online Banking Platform | Web portal for customer banking services | Critical |
| Mobile Banking Services | Mobile application for digital banking | Critical |
| Payment Processing | Processing digital and card payments | Critical |
| Fraud Detection | Monitoring and detection of fraudulent transactions | High |
| ATM Network | Customer access to cash withdrawal services | High |

Disruption of these services could significantly impact customers and banking operations.

---

# 4. Impact Categories

The Business Impact Analysis evaluates potential impacts across several dimensions.

## Financial Impact

Service disruption may lead to financial losses due to failed transactions, operational costs, or regulatory fines.

## Operational Impact

Operational disruption may affect internal banking processes, customer support services, and transaction processing.

## Reputational Impact

Extended service outages or security incidents may damage customer trust and brand reputation.

## Regulatory Impact

Financial institutions must comply with strict regulatory requirements. System outages may trigger regulatory investigations or penalties.

---

# 5. Recovery Objectives

Recovery objectives define the maximum acceptable downtime and data loss for critical services.

## Recovery Time Objective (RTO)

RTO represents the maximum acceptable time required to restore a system after disruption.

## Recovery Point Objective (RPO)

RPO represents the maximum acceptable amount of data loss measured in time.

NovaTrust Bank establishes recovery objectives for critical services as follows:

| System | Recovery Time Objective (RTO) | Recovery Point Objective (RPO) |
|------|-------------------------------|-------------------------------|
| Core Banking System | 1 hour | 15 minutes |
| Payment Processing Platform | 30 minutes | 5 minutes |
| Online Banking Platform | 2 hours | 30 minutes |
| Mobile Banking Services | 2 hours | 30 minutes |
| Fraud Detection System | 1 hour | 10 minutes |

These recovery objectives ensure that financial operations can be restored quickly following disruption.

---

# 6. Service Dependencies

Critical banking services depend on several underlying technology components.

Dependencies include:

• core banking databases  
• cloud infrastructure services  
• network connectivity  
• authentication systems  
• payment gateway integrations  

Understanding these dependencies helps NovaTrust Bank prioritize system recovery during incidents.

---

# 7. Impact of Cybersecurity Incidents

Cybersecurity incidents such as ransomware attacks, distributed denial-of-service (DDoS) attacks, or system compromise can disrupt banking operations.

Examples of possible disruption scenarios include:

• ransomware affecting core banking systems  
• DDoS attack targeting online banking services  
• compromise of payment processing infrastructure  

The Business Impact Analysis helps determine how quickly these systems must be restored to minimize operational impact.

---

# 8. Recovery Prioritization

Based on the Business Impact Analysis, NovaTrust Bank prioritizes recovery of systems according to business criticality.

Recovery priority order:

1. Payment Processing Systems  
2. Core Banking Systems  
3. Fraud Detection Systems  
4. Online Banking Platforms  
5. Mobile Banking Services  
6. ATM Infrastructure

This prioritization ensures that the most critical financial services are restored first during major incidents.

---

# 9. Integration with Disaster Recovery Planning

The results of the Business Impact Analysis support NovaTrust Bank’s disaster recovery and business continuity planning.

Disaster recovery strategies include:

• redundant cloud infrastructure  
• automated failover systems  
• secure backup and recovery processes  
• incident response procedures

These measures ensure that NovaTrust Bank can restore essential services rapidly during cyber incidents or operational disruptions.

---

# 10. Continuous Review of Business Impact Analysis

Business services and technology infrastructure evolve over time. NovaTrust Bank therefore reviews and updates the Business Impact Analysis periodically.

Review activities include:

• reassessment of critical services  
• evaluation of new systems and dependencies  
• updates to recovery objectives  
• alignment with evolving cyber threats

Maintaining an up-to-date BIA ensures that NovaTrust Bank remains prepared for emerging operational risks.

---

# 11. Strategic Value of Business Impact Analysis

Business Impact Analysis enables NovaTrust Bank to maintain strong operational resilience.

Key benefits include:

• improved incident response planning  
• faster system recovery during disruptions  
• enhanced customer trust  
• regulatory compliance with financial resilience requirements

Through this structured approach, NovaTrust Bank ensures that critical banking services remain available even during cybersecurity incidents or infrastructure failures.
