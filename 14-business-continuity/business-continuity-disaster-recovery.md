# NovaTrust Bank
## Cybersecurity Governance Program
### Module 14 – Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery

---

# 1. Introduction

Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery (BCDR) ensure that NovaTrust Bank can maintain critical banking services during operational disruptions.

Financial institutions must maintain high levels of operational resilience because interruptions to banking systems may impact customers, financial markets, and regulatory compliance.

NovaTrust Bank maintains a structured BCDR program designed to ensure that essential financial services remain available even during major incidents such as cyber attacks, system failures, or infrastructure outages.

---

# 2. Objectives of Business Continuity

The business continuity program aims to achieve several key objectives.

These objectives include:

• maintaining availability of critical banking services  
• minimizing operational disruption during incidents  
• restoring systems within defined recovery timelines  
• protecting customer financial data  
• ensuring regulatory compliance with financial resilience requirements

These objectives support the overall operational resilience strategy of NovaTrust Bank.

---

---

# 4. Disaster Recovery Objectives

NovaTrust Bank defines recovery objectives to guide system restoration following disruptions.

## Recovery Time Objective (RTO)

RTO defines the maximum acceptable time required to restore a system after disruption.

## Recovery Point Objective (RPO)

RPO defines the maximum acceptable data loss measured in time.

Recovery objectives for critical systems include:

| System | RTO | RPO |
|------|-----|-----|
| Core Banking System | 1 hour | 15 minutes |
| Payment Processing Platform | 30 minutes | 5 minutes |
| Online Banking Platform | 2 hours | 30 minutes |
| Mobile Banking System | 2 hours | 30 minutes |

These objectives ensure that financial operations can resume rapidly after disruptions.

---

# 5. Disaster Recovery Infrastructure

NovaTrust Bank maintains redundant infrastructure to support rapid system recovery.

Recovery infrastructure includes:

• geographically separated backup data centers  
• redundant cloud environments  
• automated system failover capabilities  
• secure data backup systems

These measures reduce the risk of service disruption during system failures or cyber incidents.

---

# 6. Data Backup and Recovery

Secure backup systems ensure that critical financial data can be restored if systems are compromised.

Backup processes include:

• regular automated backups of financial databases  
• encrypted storage of backup data  
• offsite backup storage locations  
• routine testing of backup restoration processes

Backup integrity is tested periodically to ensure that recovery procedures function correctly.

---

# 7. Business Continuity Procedures

NovaTrust Bank maintains documented procedures for responding to operational disruptions.

These procedures include:

• activation of business continuity plans  
• relocation of critical operations if necessary  
• restoration of technology systems  
• communication with customers and stakeholders

Clear procedures enable rapid response during crisis situations.

---

# 8. Crisis Management Structure

During major disruptions, NovaTrust Bank activates a crisis management team responsible for coordinating recovery efforts.

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Executive Leadership | Strategic decision-making during crisis |
| CISO | Cybersecurity response coordination |
| IT Infrastructure Team | System restoration and recovery |
| Operations Team | Maintaining business services |
| Communications Team | External communication with customers |

This coordinated structure ensures effective crisis management.

---

# 9. Business Continuity Testing

NovaTrust Bank regularly tests its business continuity plans to ensure readiness for real-world incidents.

Testing activities include:

• disaster recovery simulations  
• ransomware recovery exercises  
• system failover testing  
• tabletop crisis response exercises

Regular testing ensures that recovery procedures remain effective.

---

# 10. Integration with Cybersecurity Incident Response

Business continuity planning is closely integrated with cybersecurity incident response processes.

When cyber incidents disrupt systems, the incident response team works alongside disaster recovery teams to restore operations quickly.

This integration ensures coordinated response during major cyber attacks.

---

# 11. Continuous Improvement of Resilience

Operational resilience must evolve as technology and threats change.

NovaTrust Bank continuously improves its BCDR program through:

• lessons learned from incident simulations  
• regulatory guidance updates  
• improvements in recovery technology  
• enhanced operational resilience strategies

These improvements ensure that the bank remains prepared for future disruptions.

---

# 12. Importance of Business Continuity in Financial Institutions

Maintaining operational resilience is essential for financial institutions.

Effective business continuity planning enables NovaTrust Bank to:

• maintain customer access to financial services  
• minimize financial and operational disruptions  
• protect customer financial data  
• meet regulatory expectations for operational resilience

Through strong disaster recovery capabilities, NovaTrust Bank ensures that critical banking services remain available even during major disruptions.
