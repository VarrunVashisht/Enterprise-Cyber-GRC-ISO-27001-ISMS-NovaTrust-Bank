# NovaTrust Bank
## Cybersecurity Governance Program
### Module 17 – Compliance & Security Audit Program

---

# 1. Introduction

Compliance monitoring and security audits are essential components of NovaTrust Bank's cybersecurity governance program. These activities ensure that security controls are properly implemented, policies are followed, and regulatory obligations are satisfied.

Regular audits help identify weaknesses in security controls and provide assurance that the organization's cybersecurity framework operates effectively.

NovaTrust Bank maintains a structured audit program aligned with financial regulatory requirements and international cybersecurity standards.

---

# 2. Objectives of the Compliance and Audit Program

The compliance and security audit program aims to achieve several key objectives:

• verify that cybersecurity controls are implemented effectively  
• ensure compliance with regulatory requirements  
• identify weaknesses or gaps in security practices  
• support continuous improvement of cybersecurity governance  
• provide assurance to executive leadership and regulators

These objectives help strengthen NovaTrust Bank’s overall security posture.

---

# 3. Types of Security Audits

NovaTrust Bank conducts multiple types of security audits to evaluate the effectiveness of cybersecurity controls.

## Internal Security Audits

Internal audits are conducted by the organization's internal audit or cybersecurity governance teams.

These audits review:

• security policy compliance  
• system configuration security  
• access control implementation  
• incident response procedures

Internal audits help identify issues early and enable corrective actions.

---

## External Security Audits

External audits are performed by independent third-party auditors.

External assessments may include:

• ISO 27001 certification audits  
• regulatory compliance reviews  
• security maturity assessments

External audits provide independent verification of NovaTrust Bank's cybersecurity practices.

---

# 4. Regulatory Compliance Reviews

Financial institutions must demonstrate compliance with multiple regulatory frameworks.

Compliance reviews evaluate NovaTrust Bank's adherence to:

• financial sector regulatory requirements  
• privacy and data protection laws  
• cybersecurity governance frameworks

These reviews ensure that the bank meets regulatory expectations.

---

# 5. Control Testing

Security control testing verifies that implemented security controls function as intended.

Examples of control testing activities include:

• reviewing access control permissions  
• testing incident response procedures  
• validating encryption mechanisms  
• evaluating monitoring and logging systems

Testing ensures that controls are operating effectively.

---

# 6. Vulnerability Assessments

NovaTrust Bank conducts vulnerability assessments to identify weaknesses in systems and applications.

These assessments involve:

• scanning systems for known vulnerabilities  
• evaluating software configuration weaknesses  
• identifying outdated or unpatched systems

Vulnerability assessments support proactive remediation of security issues.

---

# 7. Penetration Testing

Penetration testing simulates real cyber attacks to evaluate the bank's defenses.

Security professionals attempt to exploit vulnerabilities within the bank's systems in a controlled environment.

Penetration testing helps identify weaknesses that could be exploited by attackers.

---

# 8. Audit Reporting

Results of security audits are documented and reported to relevant stakeholders.

Audit reports typically include:

• identified security weaknesses  
• risk severity ratings  
• recommended remediation actions  
• timelines for corrective measures

These reports provide leadership with visibility into the organization's security posture.

---

# 9. Corrective Action Management

When security weaknesses are identified, NovaTrust Bank implements corrective actions to address them.

Corrective action management includes:

• assigning remediation tasks to responsible teams  
• tracking remediation progress  
• verifying that issues are resolved

This structured process ensures that security weaknesses are addressed promptly.

---

# 10. Continuous Compliance Monitoring

Compliance monitoring is an ongoing activity within NovaTrust Bank.

Continuous monitoring includes:

• periodic policy compliance reviews  
• automated compliance monitoring tools  
• monitoring of regulatory developments

This ensures that the bank remains aligned with evolving regulatory expectations.

---

# 11. Integration with Risk Management

Audit findings are integrated into NovaTrust Bank's cyber risk management processes.

Security weaknesses identified during audits may result in:

• updates to the enterprise risk register  
• implementation of new security controls  
• adjustments to risk mitigation strategies

This integration ensures that cybersecurity governance remains dynamic and responsive to emerging risks.

---

# 12. Importance of Compliance and Audit Programs

A strong compliance and audit program provides assurance that NovaTrust Bank's cybersecurity controls operate effectively.

Key benefits include:

• improved security governance  
• reduced regulatory risk  
• enhanced operational resilience  
• strengthened trust with regulators and customers

Through continuous auditing and compliance monitoring, NovaTrust Bank ensures that its cybersecurity program remains effective and aligned with industry best practices.
