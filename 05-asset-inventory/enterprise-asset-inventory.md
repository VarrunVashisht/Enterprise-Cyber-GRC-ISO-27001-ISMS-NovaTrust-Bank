# NovaTrust Bank
## Cybersecurity Governance Program
### Module 05 – Enterprise Asset Inventory

---

# 1. Introduction

Effective cybersecurity governance requires a clear understanding of the assets that must be protected. For financial institutions such as NovaTrust Bank, these assets include financial systems, customer data, digital infrastructure, and supporting technologies.

An enterprise asset inventory enables NovaTrust Bank to identify critical systems, evaluate cybersecurity risks, and implement appropriate security controls.

This inventory supports several key cybersecurity processes including:

• risk assessment  
• security control implementation  
• incident response  
• regulatory compliance  
• business continuity planning  

Maintaining an accurate asset inventory is therefore a foundational component of NovaTrust Bank's cybersecurity governance framework.

---

# 2. Asset Categories

NovaTrust Bank categorizes assets into several groups to ensure comprehensive protection of information and infrastructure.

Primary asset categories include:

• Information assets  
• Application systems  
• Technology infrastructure  
• Human resources  
• Third-party services  

Each category represents a different type of resource that supports banking operations.

---

# 3. Information Assets

Information assets represent the most critical assets within the bank because they contain sensitive financial and customer information.

Examples of information assets include:

| Information Asset | Description | Classification |
|------------------|-------------|---------------|
| Customer Financial Records | Customer account balances and transaction history | Highly Confidential |
| Payment Transaction Data | Records of digital and card payments | Highly Confidential |
| Customer Personal Data | Personal identification information | Highly Confidential |
| Fraud Monitoring Data | Fraud detection and transaction monitoring data | Confidential |
| Internal Financial Reports | Bank operational reports | Internal |

Highly confidential information assets require the strongest security protections including encryption, strict access control, and monitoring.

---

# 4. Application Systems

Application systems support the delivery of banking services to customers and internal staff.

Critical banking applications include:

| Application | Description | Criticality |
|------------|-------------|------------|
| Core Banking System | Processes financial transactions and account management | Critical |
| Online Banking Platform | Web portal used by customers for banking services | Critical |
| Mobile Banking Application | Mobile application for customer banking access | Critical |
| Payment Processing System | Handles payment transfers and card transactions | Critical |
| Fraud Detection System | Identifies suspicious transactions | High |
| Customer Relationship Management (CRM) | Manages customer information | High |

Disruption or compromise of these applications could significantly impact banking operations.

---

# 5. Infrastructure Assets

Infrastructure assets support the underlying technology environment used by NovaTrust Bank.

| Infrastructure Asset | Description | Criticality |
|---------------------|-------------|------------|
| Cloud Infrastructure (AWS) | Cloud environment hosting digital banking services | Critical |
| Data Center Servers | Physical servers supporting banking systems | Critical |
| Network Infrastructure | Routers, switches, and network security devices | High |
| Identity Management Systems | Authentication and access control systems | Critical |
| Backup Systems | Data backup and recovery systems | Critical |

These infrastructure assets ensure reliable operation of banking services.

---

# 6. Human Assets

Human assets represent individuals who have access to organizational systems and data.

Key groups include:

• bank employees  
• IT administrators  
• cybersecurity analysts  
• contractors and consultants  

Human assets are important because insider threats and human error can impact cybersecurity.

Access to sensitive systems is controlled through identity and access management processes.

---

# 7. Third-Party Assets

NovaTrust Bank relies on several third-party service providers to support banking operations.

Examples include:

| Vendor | Service Provided | Risk Level |
|------|-----------------|-----------|
| Cloud Provider | Cloud hosting infrastructure | High |
| Payment Processing Vendor | Payment transaction processing | High |
| Software Vendors | Banking application software | Medium |
| Managed IT Services | Infrastructure support services | Medium |

Third-party assets introduce supply chain risks that must be managed through third-party risk management processes.

---

# 8. Asset Ownership

Each asset within NovaTrust Bank must have a clearly defined owner responsible for its security and management.

Asset owners are responsible for:

• ensuring assets are properly classified  
• implementing security controls  
• monitoring asset security risks  
• supporting incident response activities  

Clear asset ownership ensures accountability within the cybersecurity governance framework.

---

# 9. Asset Criticality and Risk Impact

Assets are classified based on their importance to banking operations.

Critical assets include systems or data that would cause major disruption if compromised.

Examples of critical assets include:

• core banking systems  
• payment processing infrastructure  
• customer financial databases  

These assets receive the highest level of security protection.

---

# 10. Asset Inventory Management

NovaTrust Bank maintains an up-to-date inventory of all critical assets.

Asset inventory management includes:

• identifying new assets introduced into the environment  
• tracking changes to existing assets  
• reviewing asset ownership and classification  
• updating the asset register regularly  

Maintaining an accurate inventory ensures that cybersecurity risks can be effectively managed.

---

# 11. Importance of Asset Inventory

A well-maintained asset inventory enables NovaTrust Bank to:

• identify critical systems requiring protection  
• prioritize cybersecurity investments  
• support risk assessment activities  
• enable faster incident response  
• ensure regulatory compliance  

Understanding organizational assets is a critical step in building an effective cybersecurity governance program.
