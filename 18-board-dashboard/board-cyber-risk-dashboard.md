# NovaTrust Bank
## Cybersecurity Governance Program
### Module 18 – Board Cyber Risk Dashboard

---

# 1. Introduction

Executive leadership and board members require clear visibility into the organization's cybersecurity posture. A cyber risk dashboard provides a concise overview of security risks, operational performance, and emerging threats affecting the organization.

NovaTrust Bank maintains a board-level cyber risk dashboard to ensure that senior leadership remains informed about cybersecurity risks and the effectiveness of security controls.

The dashboard translates technical cybersecurity information into business-level insights that support strategic decision-making.

---

# 2. Objectives of the Board Cyber Risk Dashboard

The board cyber risk dashboard aims to achieve the following objectives:

• provide executive visibility into cybersecurity risks  
• highlight critical security threats and vulnerabilities  
• monitor performance of security controls  
• support strategic cybersecurity investment decisions  
• enable effective governance of cyber risk

These insights allow leadership to understand how cybersecurity risks may affect the bank’s operations and reputation.

# 11. Importance of Executive Cyber Risk Reporting

Executive cybersecurity reporting ensures that leadership remains informed about cyber risks that may impact business operations.

A well-structured cyber risk dashboard enables NovaTrust Bank to:

• improve strategic cybersecurity governance  
• prioritize security investments  
• strengthen operational resilience  
• maintain regulatory compliance

Through clear and consistent reporting, NovaTrust Bank ensures that cybersecurity remains a key component of enterprise risk management.
