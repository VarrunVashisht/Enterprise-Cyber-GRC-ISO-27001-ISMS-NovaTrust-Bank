# NovaTrust Bank
## Cybersecurity Governance Program
### Module 07 – Enterprise Cyber Risk Assessment

---

# 1. Introduction

Cyber risk assessment is a structured process used to identify, evaluate, and prioritize cybersecurity risks that may impact NovaTrust Bank’s operations, customers, and financial systems.

Financial institutions face an evolving threat landscape including cybercrime, fraud, ransomware, insider threats, and supply chain attacks. Effective risk assessment allows NovaTrust Bank to understand these threats and implement appropriate security controls.

The objective of this cyber risk assessment is to:

• identify cyber risks affecting NovaTrust Bank  
• evaluate likelihood and impact of potential threats  
• prioritize risks based on severity  
• support risk treatment planning and security investments

This risk assessment aligns with ISO 27001 risk management requirements and the NIST Cybersecurity Framework.

---

# 2. Risk Assessment Methodology

NovaTrust Bank uses a structured risk assessment methodology based on industry best practices.

The risk evaluation process includes:

1. Asset identification  
2. Threat identification  
3. Vulnerability assessment  
4. Likelihood evaluation  
5. Impact analysis  
6. Risk scoring and prioritization

Each identified risk is assigned a score based on likelihood and business impact.

---

# 3. Risk Scoring Model

NovaTrust Bank evaluates cyber risks using a numerical scoring model.

Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact

---

## Likelihood Scale

| Score | Likelihood Description |
|------|-----------------------|
| 1 | Rare – Highly unlikely to occur |
| 2 | Unlikely – Possible but uncommon |
| 3 | Possible – Could occur occasionally |
| 4 | Likely – Expected to occur periodically |
| 5 | Very Likely – Frequently observed threat |

---

## Impact Scale

| Score | Impact Description |
|------|-------------------|
| 1 | Minimal operational impact |
| 2 | Minor service disruption |
| 3 | Moderate operational impact |
| 4 | Major service disruption or financial loss |
| 5 | Critical impact affecting core banking services |

---

# 4. Risk Severity Levels

Based on the risk score, risks are categorized into severity levels.

| Risk Score | Risk Level |
|-----------|------------|
| 1 – 5 | Low |
| 6 – 10 | Medium |
| 11 – 15 | High |
| 16 – 25 | Critical |

Critical risks require immediate mitigation.

# 6. Key Cyber Risk Categories

Cyber risks identified within NovaTrust Bank fall into several categories.

## Identity and Access Risks

Unauthorized access to banking systems due to compromised credentials or weak authentication controls.

## Infrastructure Risks

Failures or vulnerabilities within cloud infrastructure, servers, or network systems.

## Application Security Risks

Security vulnerabilities in banking applications, APIs, or software platforms.

## Third-Party Risks

Security vulnerabilities introduced by vendors or external service providers.

## Insider Threat Risks

Risks associated with employees or contractors abusing legitimate system access.

---

# 7. Risk Prioritization

Critical risks requiring immediate mitigation include:

• ransomware targeting core banking systems  
• phishing attacks leading to credential compromise  
• payment fraud attacks  
• denial-of-service attacks affecting banking services

These risks receive priority attention within NovaTrust Bank’s cybersecurity strategy.

---

# 8. Integration with Risk Management Framework

Cyber risk assessment is integrated with NovaTrust Bank’s enterprise risk management framework.

Risk assessment outputs are used to:

• inform cybersecurity investment decisions  
• guide security control implementation  
• support regulatory compliance activities  
• enable executive cyber risk reporting

The cyber risk register is reviewed regularly to ensure alignment with evolving threats.

---

# 9. Continuous Risk Monitoring

Cyber risks evolve continuously due to changes in technology and the threat landscape.

NovaTrust Bank maintains ongoing monitoring through:

• threat intelligence analysis  
• security monitoring systems  
• vulnerability assessments  
• internal security reviews

Regular reassessment ensures that new risks are identified and managed proactively.

---

# 10. Importance of Cyber Risk Assessment

Cyber risk assessment enables NovaTrust Bank to maintain a proactive cybersecurity strategy.

Key benefits include:

• prioritizing security investments  
• improving threat detection capabilities  
• strengthening operational resilience  
• supporting regulatory compliance

Through structured risk assessment, NovaTrust Bank ensures that cybersecurity efforts focus on the most significant threats facing the organization.
